**奋斗不喧哗，成功自有声。**
Work hard in silence， let your success be your noise.
————————————————

**这个世界里，虽然没有最美好的相遇，却应该有为了相遇或者重逢，所做的最美好的努力。**
In this world, although there is no best meeting, there should be the best efforts to meet or meet again.
